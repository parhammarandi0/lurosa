About

Built around the chair, not the trend cycle.

Lurosa was founded on a simple idea — that good hair starts with listening. We take time at the start of every appointment to understand your hair, your routine, and what you actually want to wake up to. The work follows from there.

Our team trains continuously in cutting and color technique, and we use professional-grade products chosen for performance, scalp health, and clean formulations. The space is small by design — calm, unhurried, and yours for the time you're with us.

  • Precision

    Cuts engineered to your hair pattern, face shape, and the way you actually style.

  • Custom color

    Tones mixed at the bowl. No off-the-shelf formulas, no shortcuts.

  • Care

    Scalp- and strand-first products that keep hair healthy between visits.
